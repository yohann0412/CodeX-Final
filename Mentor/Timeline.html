Timeline

This is a timeline of the events of CodeX 2021:
September 20th: Registrations open
December 5th: Registrations close, Opening Ceremony takes place
December 6th: Google Workshop
December 7th: Microsoft Workshop
December 8th: Questions released for the Website Development Challenge and the Problem Solving Competition. The solutions to the Problem Solving Competition are to be submitted on this day itself.
December 8th - December 11th: Evaluation of solutions to the Problem Solving Competition.
December 12th: Submission date for the Website Development Challenge.
December 12th - December 15th: Judging of the Website Development Challenge.
December 16th: Announcement of results and closing ceremony.
